Infrastructure of AI

I'm Mike S Ncube, an AI Infrastructure Engineer who bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-world enterprise deployment. My journey began in cloud architecture, evolved through data engineering, and culminated in designing production-ready AI systems that work at scale.

I don't just integrate APIs — I architect entire AI pipelines from vector ingestion to agent orchestration, from retrieval logic to cloud failover. My systems have served users across 26 countries, processed over 10 million records per day, and reduced operational latency by 60%.

My focus is on RAG architectures that retrieve with precision, agentic systems that reason through complex multi-step workflows, and AWS cloud infrastructure that scales without compromise — built for enterprise environments where reliability, compliance, and performance are non-negotiable.
